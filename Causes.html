What Sank Ships on the Great Lakes?

The height of each band shows what share of that decade's shipwrecks were caused by each factor. Select a lake to see how the mix of causes shifted over time.

Only wrecks with identifiable causes are included. "Sank / Foundered / Missing" and "Unknown" are excluded because they describe outcomes rather than causes. Percentages are of known-cause wrecks within each decade; raw counts vary significantly by decade.
